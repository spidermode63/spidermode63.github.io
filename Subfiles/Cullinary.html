I just made some granola stuff and it's pretty good

Ingredients:

  1. 1.5 cups Old Fashioned Oats
  2. 0.125 cups Oil
  3. 0.125 cups honey or maple syrup
  4. 0.125 cups brown sugar
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. 0.125 cup milk if you want less flavor for some reason.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat to 325 degrees Fahrenheight
  2. Use a cookie sheet with parchment paper
  3. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl
  4. Unceramoniously dump it all into the cookie sheet (on top of the parchment paper)
  5. Bake for about 10-20 minutes and stop and stir occasionally.
  6. Put in little bag and eat sometime when feel bad
  7. You should have enough to last you roughly one painful weekend campout :)
  8. For extra painful outings, sprinkle some sugar in and shake the bag
