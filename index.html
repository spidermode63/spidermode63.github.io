This information is being transmitted through an invisible form of light, known as radio. Your computational devices can see this light, and translate it into something valuable. It is up to you how you sail the oceans of CS, but be warned, there are sirens. They will steal your time, attention, and money if you listen to their songs. Clickbait, drugs, scams, porn, and feed-based social medias are common examples of these sirens. You have been warned.
